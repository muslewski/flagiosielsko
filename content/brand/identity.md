- **Tax ID (NIP):** PL 5541105954

## Positioning statement (derived from current copy)

> Polish family-run flag manufacturer with 30+ years of expertise, serving domestic and international clients from its base in the Kujavia-Pomerania region. Specialized in corporate, national, municipal, and club flags using high-quality materials with rigorous attention to every order regardless of size.

4. **Event organizers** — banners, advertising flags
5. **Individuals** — Polish national flags, decorative, occasional

## Tone of voice (current)

- Formal Polish ("firma rodzinna", "pozycja znacząca")
- Craft-focused, technical (mentions materials by gramatura, technique by name)
- Restrained — no marketing fluff, no exclamation marks
- Emphasizes longevity and care

**Recommended evolution:** Retain craft authority and restraint; modernize sentence rhythm; introduce more warmth on About page.

## Visual identity (currently weak)

- **Logo:** None visible as a graphic mark — just text "J-ART Flagi Osielsko" as a header
- **Color palette:** Site uses WordPress default theme with hints of red (Polish flag association is implicit)
- **Typography:** Default WordPress theme fonts
- **Imagery:** One stylized hero illustration (`Obrazek-z-flagami.png`) + 32 thumbnail photos in a gallery

**Redesign opportunity:** Build a proper visual identity. Recommended palette directions:
- **Heritage option:** Polish red (#DC143C) + deep navy + cream — patriotic, traditional
- **Craft option:** Earth tones (terracotta, ivory, ink) — emphasizes manufacturing heritage
- **Modern B2B:** Saturated red accent on near-black + off-white — clean, contemporary

## Production techniques (vocabulary to maintain)

These are technical terms the brand uses — keep verbatim in product copy:

- **Sitodruk** — screen printing
- **Sublimacja** — sublimation digital print
- **Druk cyfrowy** — digital print
- **Technika mieszana** — mixed technique
- **Wszywane** — sewn-on appliqué
- **Frontlit** — coated PVC banner material (500 g/m²)
- **Mesh / siatka** — mesh banner material (330 g/m²)
- **Satyna** — satin (used for cabinet flags, pennants)
- **Poliester 110–120 g/m²** — standard flag material

## Materials & specs (recurring across products)

- Flag fabric: poliester 110–120 g/m²
- Wind tolerance: up to 60 km/h
- Edge finishing: karabińczyki plastikowe, kółka plandekowe, troki, tunel
- Cabinet flags: dwustronna połyskliwa satyna + gold/silver eyelets
- Production lead time: minimum 4 business days
